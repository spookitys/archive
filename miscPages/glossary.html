Glossary

General Database Terms

Terms often used within ARCHIVE's database.

ARCHIVE: The organization created to contain, study, and combat unusual instances, acronymized as "Anomalous Retention & Containment Hierarchy for Irregular Violators & Esoterica".
Can be also be used as to list a documented instance within the organization's records that are usually followed by a instance identifier (Example: ARCHIVE #56)

Anomaly: Instance(s) that violate established natural laws or do not follow baseline reality.

Instance: Any place, object, person, creature, thought, entity, etc.

Documented Instance: An instance that has been documented within the ARCHIVE database, does not have to be anomalous.

Cosmological Terms

Terms often used to describe cosmology within known existence.

World: Also known as a "Game" or "Experience" to some, a players creation that is created by Roblox Studio and has some form of content within it that can be experienced by others via Genesis.

The Null Zone: A naturally generating space within a players world that is causes instability and anomalies, generates ~1,000,000 studs away from the world origin and increasing grows in intensity the further Null Zone is away from the World Spawn.

World Spawn: The necessary instance that worlds must have where accessing players materialize when respawning or entering the world.

Robloxia: Also known as "the Universe", an ever expanding continuum that encompasses almost every player generated world.

Robloxian: Any person from or residing in Robloxia.

Genesis: Also known as "the Hub" or "Project Genesis", a place in Robloxia built by Roblox HQ that is the center of every link that all worlds have, used to transport players between worlds instantly.

Realms: Worlds that exist outside Robloxia, they are non-euclidean and most likely infinite. Only 4 realms have been discovered to this date.

Hypospace: The space between worlds within Robloxia, often unstable and anomalous.

(The) Banlands: A realm that consists of mountanous terrain and a ocean of Robloxian corpses, used by the organization to trap the most dangerous anomalies and offenders.

Organization Terms

Terms often used by the organization as a whole.

Division: A branch of the organization that has a specific role in the organization, there are 12 divisions in total, each having one BoD member.

Board of Dominance: Also known as BoD, a body within the Administrative Division that contains all 12 of their division leads, this group holds authority over all branches within the organization, only under The Monitor.

The Monitor: A sole person with absolute power within the organization, most likely the founder of the organziation, no other information is known or needed.

CAOS: An acronym for "Containment and Alignment Operational Soldiers" (acryonym pronounced /kāˌäs/). Refers to a group within the Containment Division, comprised of many skilled and often genetically enchanced soldiers with access to standard issue ARCHIVE weapons, they are deployed to counter and contain anomalies.

GUEST: An acronym for "Genetically Unfit Expendable Surrogate Templates". Refers to instance(s) of humanoid creatures created by "The Flesh Forge" (See ARCHIVE #45), often used for experimental or dangerous testing as "cannon fodder".
