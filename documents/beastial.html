Description

#0004, designated “Beastial” by ARCHIVE classification, is an approximately 8-foot-tall humanoid entity exhibiting near-human anatomical proportions with significant biological deviations.

Dermal composition consists of dense, scale-like plating resistant to conventional ballistic penetration. Muscular fibers demonstrate tensile strength comparable to Kevlar-grade material. Skeletal and internal structures exhibit similarly elevated durability. Ocular organs emit a persistent red luminescence, believed to be associated with enhanced visual processing.

#0004 displays extreme physical capabilities. Controlled facility testing confirms the ability to lift loads up to 5,000 kg, achieve sustained speeds of 36.2 studs per second, and endure in excess of 50 consecutive hours of high-intensity combat engagement without structural or metabolic collapse.

Despite these capabilities, #0004 remains one of the few high-tier anomalies responsive to 0X-M8 (“MINDBRINGER”). Properly administered dosages result in behavioral stabilization and compliance, enabling controlled deployment as an ARCHIVE operational asset.

Following successful subjugation, ARCHIVE has augmented #0004’s operational efficiency through structured enhancement protocols, including Baritonium injections to elevate physical output, formalized combat training, endurance conditioning, tactical simulation exposure, and assignment of specialized equipment.

Issued equipment includes reinforced constud-plated armor rated to withstand impacts equivalent to 150 megatons of TNT without structural compromise. #0004 has also been equipped with a specialized hammer constructed from a constud-voidium alloy. The voidium component, forged within the outer layer of #0002-A (“The Void Star”), allows conversion of applied kinetic force into directed energy projections upon impact.

Nutritional intake consists primarily of raw meat and blood. Metabolic efficiency is unusually high; sustenance requirements average one serving of meat every four weeks and one pint of blood approximately every five months.

#0004 was discovered unconscious in deep Hypospace during a reconnaissance operation. Upon revival, the entity engaged nearby researchers and CAOS personnel in hostile behavior. Following brief containment engagement, #0004 demonstrated neutral disposition toward ARCHIVE personnel and has since remained conditionally cooperative.

Origin remains undetermined. Linguistic capability is minimal; communication is limited to basic nonverbal indicators and fragmented symbolic responses. ARCHIVE xenospatial analysis suggests #0004’s species originated from a planetary body destroyed by supernova. No additional members of the species have been located.

Containment Protocol

#0004 is housed within a low-risk constud containment chamber at Sanctum 6. Chamber dimensions must allow for unrestricted movement while preventing structural stress against containment boundaries.

Personnel observation is permitted. Direct interaction requires a minimum escort of two CAOS guards and prior authorization from Rank 4 or above.

#0004 is to receive a 3 mg injection of 0X-M8 (“MINDBRINGER”) every four days. Administration must be conducted by trained CAOS medical personnel. Following injection, the containment sector is to remain acoustically minimal for a duration of 20 minutes to prevent agitation or adverse neural stimulation.

If deployed for field operations, #0004 is to be transported within a reinforced constud restraint cage via ARCHIVE heavy-lift apparatus. A minimum of three CAOS guards must accompany transport. Post-operation, #0004 is to be re-administered 0X-M8 prior to return to Sanctum 6 containment.

In the event of 0X-M8 resistance or behavioral escalation, emergency suppression measures including high-output containment barriers and Baritonium inhibitors are authorized.
