Description

The Rejected Spawn is an anomalous Spawn location within #0001-B. It emits a constant dim red glow, approximately 100 lumens, that pulses approximately every 42 seconds to an eye burning 10,000 lumens. Any person that perceives the glow outside 100 studs experiences intense visual discomfort and disorientation.

The world that houses the Rejected Spawn is currently not known for any anomalous effects exempt from the spawn itself, the world has been given the designation #0001-B, and the spawn itself has been given the designation #0001-A. The world was created by the user "LucasTheCool_55" on December 17th, 2007. The world features a studded baseplate of standard dimensions, a classic Roblox starter house 12 studs west of world origin, a slate statue of a humanoid figure with no remarkable distinctions 55 studs north of world origin, and the spawn itself at the center of the world.

Perceiving #0001-A's pulse within a 100 stud radius gives any living organism a strong desire and enticement to come into contact with the spawn. This desire is permanent and mind altering until contact with #0001-A is met, brain scans of GUESTs during perception of #0001-A show complete neural overload of the ventral striatum and muffling of the temporal lobe's language area, making all who perceive #0001-A unable to register orders or commands. If a person is physically unable to reach #0001-A, they begin to experience deep depression, violent tendencies, and high levels of insanity. These side effects last for approximately 23 minutes before they suffer a fatal brain stroke.

If a person comes into contact with #0001-A, with or without the perceived desire, they are immediately set with a bright red fire, intensely burning them and pulverizing them within seconds. Within the standard respawn time of 5 seconds, the person materializes back at the spawn, only to be burnt again with the red fire. This loop is eternal and cannot be broken unless the person is killed using force outside the spawn, such as ARCHIVE weapons. If the person experienced the deep desire to come in contact with #0001-A, they appear to lose it once they are in the cycle, and experience intense pain similar to pertaining constant 2nd degree burns. Every time a person burns within #0001-A, the fire around #0001-A grows larger by an unknown small amount.

Attempts to extinguish the flame have proven unsuccessful, the flame is anomalously resistant to water, extinguishing agents, smothering, and any other traditional forms of extinguishing fire. The red glow also appears to bypass any visual blocks, including light dampening goggles, blindfolds, face shields, looking away, etc.. the only proven way to not inhibit its effects are to be outside of its 100 stud range when it pulses.

The Rejected Spawn was discovered by patrolling personnel on ███ ███, ████. Personnel found 102 Robloxians trapped within the eternal loop of #0001-A, after 24 days of experimenting with the Robloxians, they were terminated and #0001-B was locked down and cut off from Genesis's portal navigation under the guise of an backdoor virus.

#0001-A and #0001-B have both been moved to Sanctum 16's world repository wing 34 days after discovery.

Containment Protocol

Access to the world repository of #0001-B has been limited to personnel rank 4 and above, any experiments using #0001-A are to only be using GUEST personnel, and are to execute them after a 5 day period at the maximum. The world repository #0001-A gate is to be encased within hyperfire retardant plates 15x15x3 studs in dimensions. 3 CAOS guards are to monitor and report any breaches or leaks to rank 3 and above. Any researchers conducting experiments within #0001-B are to be in a reinforced constud viewing area 200 studs away from #0001-A, hyperfire surveillance cameras are to be placed on each corner of the constud room facing #0001-A and should be monitored hourly to ensure integrity. Any personnel entering #0001-B must be checked and expunged of any flammable material on their person.
