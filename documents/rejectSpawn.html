Description

#0001-A, designated “The Rejected Spawn,” is an anomalous spawn location situated within world instance #0001-B. The entity emits a continuous red glow measuring approximately 100 lumens. At intervals of approximately 42 seconds, luminosity intensifies to an estimated 10,000 lumens before returning to baseline. Visual exposure to the pulse from beyond a 100-stud radius results in acute ocular discomfort and spatial disorientation.

World instance #0001-B has not demonstrated anomalous properties independent of #0001-A. The world consists of a standard studded baseplate, a classic starter house positioned 12 studs west of world origin, a slate humanoid statue lacking distinguishing characteristics 55 studs north of origin, and #0001-A located centrally. World instance #0001-B was created by user “LucasTheCool_55” on December 17, 2007.

Exposure to #0001-As pulse within a 100-stud radius induces a persistent compulsive drive to establish physical contact with the entity. This compulsion overrides executive reasoning and persists until contact is achieved. Neural scans conducted on GUEST-class subjects indicate extreme stimulation of the ventral striatum and suppression of temporal language-processing regions, rendering subjects nonresponsive to external commands or directives.

If a subject is physically prevented from reaching #0001-A, progressive psychological destabilization occurs, including severe depressive symptoms, violent behavioral impulses, and advanced cognitive deterioration. These symptoms persist for approximately 23 minutes, after which subjects experience fatal cerebral failure.

Direct physical contact with #0001-A results in immediate ignition via anomalous red flame. Subjects are incinerated and structurally disintegrated within seconds. Following the standard respawn interval of five seconds, subjects rematerialize at #0001-A and are re-ignited. This combustion cycle repeats indefinitely. Termination of the cycle has only been achieved through external lethal intervention using ARCHIVE weaponry.

Subjects previously affected by compulsive drive lose the compulsion upon entry into the combustion cycle and instead exhibit sustained pain responses consistent with continuous fourth-degree burn trauma. Each combustion event produces a minor but measurable increase in flame volume surrounding #0001-A. Flame expansion subsides once the subject is removed from the cycle.

Extinguishment attempts have failed. The flame demonstrates resistance to water, chemical suppressants, oxygen deprivation, and conventional extinguishing methodologies, setting it as a class 2 Hyperfire . The emitted red pulse bypasses conventional visual obstructions, including protective lenses, blindfolds, and averted gaze. Avoidance of exposure is only reliable beyond the 100-stud pulse radius.

#0001-A was identified by patrolling personnel on ███ ███, ████. One hundred and two individuals were discovered within the combustion cycle upon discovery. Following 24 days of controlled experimentation, all affected individuals were terminated. World instance #0001-B was subsequently isolated and removed from Genesis portal navigation under the guise of a backdoor virus infection.

#0001-A and #0001-B were transferred to Sanctum 16, World Repository Wing, 34 days post-discovery.

4 patrolling CAOS units were under an ongoing investigation on anomalous worlds, after 16 days of searching on their designated sector, they found world #0001-B with the one hundred and two individuals within the combustion cycle.

(#0001-A pulses, CAOS Unit 3 and 4 were within the 100 stud pulse radius, initiating the compulsion drive.)
CAOS Unit 1: (Visual confusion) █████? What are you doing? [Expletive]— stay back!
(CAOS Units 3 and 4 remain unresponsive, lifelessly marching towards #0001-A)
(CAOS Units 1 and 2 attempt to grab Units 3 and 4, the latter shakes their grasp away, continuing to march)
CAOS Unit 2, to Units 3 and 4: Are you not seeing what's happening to those people, what's wrong with you?!
(After numerous attempts to intervene, Units 1 and 2 are forced to retreat and report back to ARCHIVE)

After the incident, the remaining CAOS units were debriefed and the anomaly was contained.

Containment Protocol

Access to world instance #0001-B is restricted to Rank 4 personnel and above. Experimental interaction with #0001-A is limited exclusively to GUEST-class subjects. Subjects are to be terminated no later than five days following induction into the combustion cycle.

The repository access gate to #0001-B shall remain encased in Hyperfire-retardant plating measuring 15 x 15 x 3 studs. Two CAOS-designated security personnel are to maintain continuous surveillance and report structural compromise or anomalous deviation to Rank 3 authority without delay.

All observational research must be conducted from a reinforced constud viewing chamber positioned no closer than 200 studs from #0001-A. Hyperfire-resistant surveillance systems are to be installed at each corner of the chamber with direct visual alignment to the entity. Surveillance integrity is to be verified hourly.

Personnel entering #0001-B are subject to inspection and removal of all flammable materials prior to authorization of entry.
