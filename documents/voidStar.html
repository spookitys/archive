Description

#0002, designated “The Void Star,” is a region of condensed Hypospace within the deep regions of Robloxia. Internal mass exceeds the Swartzchild radius of the area, however no singularity is formed for unknown anomalous reasons. #0002 yields multiple anomalous properties, the most apparent visible one is the fact that all light appears to be absorbed within its vicinity, creating a void-like effect.

Containment Event Log ▼

4 patrolling CAOS units were under an ongoing investigation on anomalous worlds, after 16 days of searching on their designated sector, they found world #0001-B with the one hundred and two individuals within the combustion cycle.

CAOS Unit 1: Report immediately, it appears that this world has an anomaly.
CAOS Unit 2: Copy, transporting as soon as possible.
(CAOS units arrive at #0001-B, hearing the blood curtling screaming of dozens of Robloxians)
CAOS Unit 3: What the [Expletive]—
CAOS Unit 1: Stay.. keep within protocol, we need to contain this and report it back to Sanctum 16.
(#0001-A pulses, CAOS Unit 3 and 4 were within the 100 stud pulse radius, initiating the compulsion drive.)
CAOS Unit 1: (Visual confusion) █████? What are you doing? [Expletive]— stay back!
(CAOS Units 3 and 4 remain unresponsive, lifelessly marching towards #0001-A)
(CAOS Units 1 and 2 attempt to grab Units 3 and 4, the latter shakes their grasp away, continuing to march)
CAOS Unit 2, to Units 3 and 4: Are you not seeing what's happening to those people, what's wrong with you?!
(After numerous attempts to intervene, Units 1 and 2 are forced to retreat and report back to ARCHIVE)

After the incident, the remaining CAOS units were debriefed and the anomaly was contained.

Containment Protocol

Access to world instance #0001-B is restricted to Rank 4 personnel and above. Experimental interaction with #0001-A is limited exclusively to GUEST-class subjects. Subjects are to be terminated no later than five days following induction into the combustion cycle.

The repository access gate to #0001-B shall remain encased in Hyperfire-retardant plating measuring 15 x 15 x 3 studs. Two CAOS guards are to maintain continuous surveillance and report structural compromise or anomalous deviation to Rank 3 authority without delay.

All observational research must be conducted from a reinforced constud viewing chamber positioned no closer than 200 studs from #0001-A. Hyperfire-resistant surveillance systems are to be installed at each corner of the chamber with direct visual alignment to the entity. Surveillance integrity is to be verified hourly.

Personnel entering #0001-B are subject to inspection and removal of all flammable materials prior to authorization of entry.
