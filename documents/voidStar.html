Description

#0002-A, designated “The Void Star,” is a condensed Hypospatial mass located within the deep regions of Robloxia, measuring approximately 500,000 studs in radius. Recorded internal mass density exceeds the theoretical Schwarzschild limit for the measured volume; however, no gravitational singularity has formed. The absence of collapse is classified as anomalous.

All electromagnetic radiation entering the proximity of #0002-A is absorbed without measurable reflection or scattering, producing a visually void-like absence of light. Spectral analysis confirms total absorption across observed wavelengths.

Proximity within approximately 20,000,000 studs of #0002-A induces progressive spatial distortion and temporal desynchronization. Navigational instruments experience drift, coordinate collapse, and recursive positional error. Temporal devices exhibit measurable lag and forward-jump inconsistencies. Distortive effects intensify proportionally with decreasing distance to the entity.

Remote reconnaissance has identified five primary structural layers within #0002-A.

#0002-B, the outermost region, resembles a stellar photosphere analogue. It consists of a non-Newtonian fluid shell approximately 500 studs in thickness. Estimated viscosity measures 90,000 Saybolt Second Universals. Application of pressure exceeding approximately 203 PSI triggers instantaneous solidification. The resulting material state exceeds all known penetrative tolerances and has proven impervious to mechanical, thermal, and anomalous drilling methodologies.

#0002-C, designated “The Abyssal Storm,” extends inward approximately 100,000 studs. This region is characterized by volatile energy surges, unstable currents, and continuous structural flux. Current velocities reach several thousand studs per second. Internal mass flow readings approximate 10,000 kg/s despite the region’s gaseous classification. Mapping has been achieved; stable traversal has not.

#0002-D, designated “Perfect Nothing,” spans approximately 300,000 studs. Within this region, no particles, fields, radiation, or measurable quantum fluctuations have been detected. Boundary transition from #0002-C to #0002-D is sharply defined and remains unexplained. Only one confirmed probe entry has occurred. The Boreas-02 Research Drone was instantaneously disintegrated upon entry. Current theoretical models suggest that matter entering a perfect vacuum of this magnitude undergoes immediate structural dissipation to equalize spatial imbalance.

Unverified theoretical modeling suggests the existence of a central core within #0002-A responsible for sustaining its anomalous stability. No observational confirmation has been achieved.

#0002-E, designated “The Void Crown,” is an artifact composed of unidentified material and origin. Anomalous signature readings match those of #0002-A. Subjects who don #0002-E demonstrate immediate amplification of energy output consistent with direct channeling from #0002-A. Manifested abilities include large-scale destructive emission events capable of catastrophic infrastructural failure. Psychological destabilization occurs within seconds of synchronization. Fatal neurological collapse is typical within minutes, though few instances have proven that a "worthy" subject can don #0002-E for extended periods of time.

A civilian collective referred to as the “Void Cult” has emerged within Robloxia, exhibiting organized devotion to #0002-A and #0002-E. Intelligence suggests approximately 240 confirmed participants. Multiple invasions attempts to Sanctum 32 have been recorded. Information leakage pathways remain under investigation. Cult members do not present inherent anomalous properties but display extreme ideological fixation.

Containment Protocol

All public and private Hypospace traversal systems are to be continuously monitored. Entry within a 50,000,000 stud exclusion radius surrounding #0002-A is strictly prohibited. Any world instance detected within this radius is to be relocated or permanently terminated under the guise of server-side errors.

Navigational databases are to automatically reroute coordinates intersecting the exclusion zone. Any unauthorized attempt to manually override exclusion parameters will result in immediate account suspension and investigation by Internal Security.

#0002-E (The Void Crown) is to be contained within a reinforced constud vault measuring 20 x 20 x 5 studs. The artifact shall remain secured upon a fixed podium enclosed by Vallance Anti-Reality-Tampering Glass. The chamber must be vacuum-sealed and electromagnetically isolated to prevent signal resonance with #0002-A.

The vault is to be monitored by a minimum of four motion-detection surveillance systems operating on independent power supplies. Five CAOS security personnel are to be stationed externally at all times. An additional rapid-response unit of twenty-five CAOS personnel shall remain on permanent standby within Sanctum 32.

Under no circumstances is physical contact with #0002-E permitted outside of Rank 2 authorization. Testing is suspended indefinitely. The artifact is classified as Priority Omega-Level due to the potential for facility-wide annihilation upon synchronization with a host.

In the event of unauthorized acquisition of #0002-E, immediate escalation to Site Catastrophic Protocol is mandatory. All nonessential personnel are to evacuate. Remote neutralization systems, including high-yield ARCHIVE weaponry, are authorized without Ethics review. If subject destabilization reaches critical emission levels, Sanctum-wide self-termination procedures may be initiated to prevent crown relocation.

Information regarding #0002-E is restricted to Rank 3 and above. All references in public or semi-public networks are to be suppressed by the Obfuscation Division. Active monitoring of Void Cult communications is ongoing.
