Description

#0003-A, self-identified as “Dignity” within the Robloxian Database, is a former world developer and confirmed mass-casualty offender. Subject is primarily associated with the development of advanced sonographic weaponry (#0003-B) and unauthorized synchronization with #0002-E (“The Void Crown”).

Prior to hostile designation, #0003-A was recognized as a high-level developer affiliated with Roblox HQ. Subject demonstrated advanced scripting and environmental engineering capabilities and accumulated significant financial success through published world instances.

Before the official release of public audio uploads in 2013, #0003-A developed an independent system called “Dignity’s Music Uploader.” The system embedded external audio files directly into Roblox Studio’s internal database structure, enabling playback within hosted worlds.

Administrative review by Roblox HQ resulted in mass termination of associated worlds due to unauthorized audio distribution. All instances utilizing the uploader were removed. #0003-A incurred substantial financial loss. Public backlash from affected developers followed. Subject subsequently severed affiliation with Roblox HQ and disappeared from public networks.

Approximately several months later, #0003-A constructed #0003-B (“Sonocannon”) within an isolated private world beyond standard jurisdiction. #0003-B is a turret-like structure measuring 500 x 500 x 300 studs. Utilizing a recovered fragment of the Music Uploader system, the device amplified acoustic output beyond 300 decibels, producing matter-disruptive shockwaves capable of immediate structural annihilation upon contact.

The device also functioned reciprocally as a long-range acoustic surveillance instrument, enabling subject to intercept private communications. Through this method, #0003-A acquired intelligence regarding #0002-E during a classified meeting between the Board of Dominion and Roblox HQ administrators.

#0003-A initiated an assault on Sanctum 32, utilizing #0003-B to breach reinforced constud containment structures housing #0002-E. Casualties included 5,267 confirmed deceased personnel and 12,903 injured. Subject successfully acquired and donned #0002-E.

While synchronized with #0002-E, #0003-A annihilated Sanctum 32’s Object Repository and Self-Defense Wing. Subject exhibited extended survivability while wearing the artifact, reportedly due to elevated emotional resilience. This claim remains unverified.

Subsequently, #0003-A targeted Roblox HQ’s Genesis Portal Network infrastructure, resulting in 3,841,192 civilian fatalities over approximately two hours.

In response, the Board of Dominion authorized Protocol: “God Slayer.” After sustained engagement, #0003-A was neutralized and captured. Subject is currently contained within Sanctum 8, Offender Repository Wing. #0002-E has been re-secured within reconstructed Sanctum 32.

All civilians exposed to the Genesis event were administered Bridgid Class-3 hallucinogens using an aerosol deployer.

Containment Protocol

#0003-A is classified as Omega-Level Hostile and is to be maintained under maximum-security isolation within Sanctum 8, Offender Repository Wing.

Subject is housed within a triple-layer constud containment chamber measuring 12 x 12 x 12 studs. The chamber is acoustically dampened to prevent resonance amplification and isolated from structural load-bearing frameworks to reduce vibrational propagation risk.

A spinal anti-stud injection apparatus is affixed via reinforced torso harness. Any detected breach attempt, unauthorized movement beyond designated perimeter, or anomalous energy surge will trigger automatic injection. The compound is designed to destabilize subject’s structural integrity at a molecular level, resulting in rapid internal dissolution.

Subject is to remain under continuous audiovisual surveillance. All monitoring systems must operate on isolated power grids. No external network connectivity is permitted within 500 studs of containment.

Communication with #0003-A requires Rank 4 authorization and must occur within a remote observation chamber utilizing one-way audio relay. No acoustic amplification devices are permitted during interviews.

In the event of Crown reacquisition or anomalous energy signature consistent with #0002-E synchronization, immediate escalation to Sector Collapse Protocol is authorized. Lethal force requires no Ethics Division review.

No personnel are to discuss Genesis Portal Network reconstruction efforts within auditory range of subject.
